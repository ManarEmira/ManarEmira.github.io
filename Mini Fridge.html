MINI FRIDGE

A functional mini fridge that can cool and store a small number of items.

About Project

The mini fridge project is an innovative and useful project built using a Peltier cooling system, a temperature sensor, and an LCD screen. The project's objective was to design and build a compact, portable fridge capable of cooling and storing a small number of items.

The Peltier cooling system was chosen because of its efficiency and compact size, making it ideal for this project. The temperature sensor was incorporated into the design to allow for accurate temperature monitoring, and the LCD screen provided a convenient way to view the fridge's temperature in real-time.

The project's construction involved designing and assembling a custom enclosure to house the Peltier cooling system, temperature sensor, and LCD screen. The enclosure was designed to be compact and portable, making it easy to carry and transport.



Design

The mini-fridge was designed to be small and compact, making it perfect for personal use or for a small office. The design incorporated a Peltier cooling system, which uses an electrical current to transfer heat from one side of the device to the other. The cooling system was integrated into the mini-fridge's walls to maximize space and minimize noise.



Fabrication

The fabrication of the mini-fridge is made from MDF wood and foam using CNC. MDF wood is a popular material due to its affordability and ease of machining, while foam is used for insulation purposes to keep the temperature inside the fridge stable. The CNC machine is used to precisely cut the wood and foam to the desired shapes and sizes, ensuring that the final product is of high quality and accuracy.
